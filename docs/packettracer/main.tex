\section{Introduction}

The Smart Mosque Management System is a simulated IoT-based environment developed in Cisco Packet Tracer to improve operational efficiency, worshipper comfort, energy conservation, and water management inside mosques.

The system follows a Sense $\rightarrow$ Process $\rightarrow$ Act $\rightarrow$ Monitor operational model where sensors continuously monitor environmental conditions, the IoT controller processes sensor data, automation conditions trigger actuator responses, and administrators supervise the system through a centralized dashboard.

The project focuses on:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Smart energy management
    \item Occupancy-aware automation
    \item Environmental comfort control
    \item Water conservation
    \item Real-time monitoring
\end{itemize}

The implementation demonstrates how IoT technologies can support sustainable public facilities while reducing operational waste and improving responsiveness during high-attendance periods such as Friday prayers and Ramadan.

\section{System Topology and Infrastructure}

The simulation environment is divided into three functional zones:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Prayer Hall
    \item Wudu Area
    \item Exterior / Entrance Area
\end{itemize}

The infrastructure is built around a centralized IoT architecture where sensors, actuators, servers, and wireless access points communicate through a local network.

\subsection{Core Infrastructure Components}

The system includes:

    \item Cisco 2960-24TT switch
    \item IoT Registration Server
    \item DHCP Server

\subsection{Environmental Control}

The system monitors:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Temperature
    \item Humidity
    \item CO$_2$
\end{itemize}

Environmental automation thresholds are shown below.

\begin{table}[H]
\centering
\begin{tabular}{L{4cm}L{4cm}L{5cm}}
\toprule
\textbf{Parameter} & \textbf{Threshold} & \textbf{Action} \\
\midrule
Temperature & $>$ 28$^\circ$C & Activate fan \\
Temperature & $>$ 32$^\circ$C & Activate AC \\
Humidity & $<$ 30\% & Activate humidifier \\
Humidity & $\geq$ 60\% & Open smart window \\
CO$_2$ & High threshold exceeded & Activate ventilation \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}

\section{Challenges and Solutions}

Several implementation challenges were encountered during development.

\begin{table}[H]
\centering
\begin{tabular}{L{6cm}L{8cm}}
\toprule
\textbf{Challenge} & \textbf{Solution} \\
\midrule
No native solenoid valve in CPT & Used siren as simulation proxy \\
No external API support & Used USNO prayer-time calculations \\
Large number of devices & Implemented DHCP and DNS servers \\
Environmental testing difficulty & Used CPT environment controls \\
Server functionality limitations & Used separate infrastructure servers \\
