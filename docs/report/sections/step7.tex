\section{Functional View Specification}

\subsection{Overview}
The functional view breaks the system into independent blocks by behavior. Each block has defined inputs, outputs, and responsibilities, with clear data flows for modularity and maintainability.

\subsection{Functional Block Descriptions}

\subsubsection*{Block 1 -- Sensing Block}
Collects raw data from the mosque environment.
\begin{table}[htbp]
\centering
\begin{tabular*}{\textwidth}{@{\extracolsep{\fill}}L{4.2cm} L{2.8cm} L{2.6cm} L{4cm}}
\toprule
\textbf{Sub-function} & \textbf{Sensor} & \textbf{Interval} & \textbf{Output} \\
\midrule
Entry/exit detection & PIR (entrance) & Event-based & Increment/decrement event \\
Zone presence detection & PIR (hall zones) & Every 5s & Presence flag per zone \\
Water flow measurement & Flow sensor & Every 1s & Flow rate (L/min) \\
Tap-area presence & PIR (wudu) & Event-based & Presence flag \\
Air quality reading & IAQ sensor & Every 60s & CO$_2$, temperature, humidity \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular*}
\caption{Sensing block functions}
\end{table}

Failure behavior: Offline sensors are flagged to processing, then safe defaults are applied.

\subsubsection*{Block 2 -- Processing Block}
Decision core running on ESP32.
\begin{itemize}
  \item Occupancy counter with floor at zero.
  \item Prayer window evaluator from cached schedule.
  \item Energy decision engine for lights/fans.
  \item Air quality decision engine independent from prayer window.
  \item Water anomaly detection and valve control.
  \item State manager as single source of truth.
\end{itemize}

\subsubsection*{Block 3 -- Actuation Block}
Executes commands on relays and valve, with confirmation and retries.

Solenoid valve & OPEN/CLOSE per tap & GPIO acknowledgment \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular*}
\caption{Actuation block control and feedback}
\end{table}

\subsubsection*{Block 4 -- Data and Storage Block}
Persists time-series data for dashboard and analytics.
\begin{table}[htbp]
\centering
\begin{tabular*}{\textwidth}{@{\extracolsep{\fill}}L{4.3cm} L{5.3cm} L{4.6cm}}
\toprule
\textbf{Data Type} & \textbf{Logging Frequency} & \textbf{Retention} \\
\midrule
Occupancy count & Every 30s & 30-day rolling \\
Water usage per tap & Per session + hourly total & 30-day rolling \\
Device state changes & On every transition & 30-day rolling \\
Air quality readings & Every 60s & 30-day rolling \\
Alert records & On every alert event & 30-day rolling \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular*}
\caption{Stored operational data}
\end{table}

\subsubsection*{Block 5 -- Alert Block}
Evaluates alert conditions, deduplicates repeated alerts, and pushes active alerts to dashboard.

\subsubsection*{Block 6 -- Dashboard Block}
Provides monitoring panels and manual override controls for admin users.

\subsubsection*{Block 7 -- External API Block}
Fetches daily prayer times and caches them for resilience.
